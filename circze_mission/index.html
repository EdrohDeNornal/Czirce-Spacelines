What is Circze's Mission?

At the dawn of our history, when the ancient city still lay beneath skies that no longer exist, it was Circze who took the first step. Not by lineage, but by merit: she was the first to foresee the need for an exodus, the planting of a new seed on a distant world. It was on that second planet that our current civilization was born. Her title of Princess did not signify dominion, but origin; and her legacy transcended authority, becoming spiritual guidance for the generations to come.

Today, Circze has crossed the threshold of matter, yet her spirit endures on higher planes. Those who can perceive her speak of visions, lucid intuitions, and dreams laden with symbols. From that unseen dimension, she has initiated The Circze Mission: a silent call to gather the souls who, though unaware, are destined to uphold the balance of our galaxy—beyond the Great Attractor.

What is, then, the Circze Mission?
It is an invitation. A frequency. A silent operation to prepare the collective soul of our world for the coming Conflict— that will be set beyond the Great Attractor, that hidden region of the cosmos unknown to the people of this galaxy.

This war will not be fought with fire or iron. There will be no missiles, no shields, no plasma accelerators, no visible armies. It will be a war of spirit—a battle for consciousness. Our weapons: truth without masks, love without conditions, purity without pride, and a light that needs no eyes to be seen.

Only those with hearts still whole will be able to respond. There is no call for blood, no demand for oaths. This is an invitation—not to prove, but to reveal: clarity, will, and the capacity to observe the ego—and master it. Enlistment will not be obvious. Our methods are unknown to the children of the Milky Way. If you have been chosen, you are already being tested—even now, without knowing it.

We are the Gvatchans, A civilization driven by the quest for evolution and connection with the whole universe— a united group of races shaped by shared suffering, bound not by origin but by the fullness of their hearts, welcoming anyone who carries kindness within. Our enemies—ancient and declared—once tried to destroy us through force. They failed. So they changed their tactics: to sow discord from within, infiltrate our structures, and corrupt our childrens.

But Circze saw it. She returned—beyond time—to warn those with discernment. To transmit her plan to a prepared few. Thus was born the Circze Mission: the first move in a strategic sequence that will shape the fate of our galaxy for cycles to come.

We seek voluntary souls. Spirits willing to walk between worlds, to bear the symbol, to radiate the unseen. This is the Army of Light. The restoration of our harmony depends on it.

This is the final trial for us—the Gvatchans. The ultimate leap in our evolution. You are called to stand beside us, to fight alongside us, and to forge history at this defining crossroads of our galaxy. Your courage will be etched into eternity. Circze never forgets those who stand with her in the face of destiny
