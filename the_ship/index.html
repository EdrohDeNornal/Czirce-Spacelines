The Ship

✦The "Tiefe Erfahrung Circze" (Tieffy for short) is a Beluga Liner produced by Saud Kruger. It has nothing to envy from the ships of the Empire or the Federation.

The Tieffy—like all Saud Kruger vessels—is designed not only for luxury, but for long term comfort and well-being. She offers the amenities required for long deep-space journeys without wearing down those aboard. And unlike most pleasure liners, she has the speed to outrun more threats than she should ever have to face.

However, the Tieffy offers far more than the standard Beluga Liner. It can be modified according to the client’s taste, mission profile, or voyage requirements.

Among its features are:
A ballroom with amphitheater for social events,
A sports hall for indoor sports such as soccer, basketball, or tennis,
A Stellar Lounge,
A Galactic Bar,
A cinema,
A library,
And a flower garden—yes, a flower garden, for passengers who enjoy cultivating flora while traversing the stars.
In addition, the Tieffy is equipped with a hangar bay that supports two land vehicles, ready to explore alien planets as part of your itinerary.

You might now ask:
"Okay… it’s better than the other ships, but can it fight? Can it do a barrel roll?"
Quick answer: yes. Yes, it can.
The Beluga Liner was originally designed for tourism and is not intended for combat. However, the Tieffy has been modified for sustained defense and tactical retreat, as a last resort.
Passenger life and mental health are the pilot's top priorities.
Engagement in combat is discouraged, but evasion is not the only option.

Tieffy features:
A reinforced hull,
Modified thrusters and shielding systems (most Belugas in the Bubble don't even equip shields),
Six hardpoints available for turreted weapons and ordinance,
Personal weapons and cyborgs onboard in case of pirate boarding.
Yes, if needed—we can plan an assault with this ship.
But its most important tactical asset is the Ship-Launched Fighter Module (SLF), equipped with:
2 fighters (8 resupplies each),
3 Commanders from the Pilots Federation, providing steady, proven firepower against pirates and space raiders.
Your safety is not only assured—it's enforced.
